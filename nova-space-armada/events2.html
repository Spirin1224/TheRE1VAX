Space mobilization is an [event](https://novaspacearmada.fandom.com/wiki/Events "Events") with a collection of 7 events (some are not unique) that rotate throughout the week. This mobilization has 6 milestones per event, and these milestones are a great source of resources like GEC for the free to play player. There is a leaderboard that comes with this event - the daily leaderboard that reflects the current event, and the weekly leaderboard, which reflects the total points in an entire week. Both these leaderboards will come with rewards for making it into the top 100.
